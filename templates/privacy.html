Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Prompt Insights handles your information:

1. Information Collection

We only collect the prompts you input to provide feedback and analysis. No personal identifying information is required or stored.

2. Data Usage

Your inputs are used solely for generating prompt evaluations and are not stored or shared with third parties.

3. Data Retention

Temporary data may be stored locally during your session. We do not retain prompt history.

4. Cookies

This app does not use cookies or trackers by default. If integrated with third-party analytics in the future, you will be notified.

5. Contact

If you have privacy concerns, please contact us at promptinsight32@gmail.com.
