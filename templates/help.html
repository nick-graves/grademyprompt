Help & Usage Guide

Step 1: Enter your AI prompt in the input field and choose a model.

Step 2: Click "Grade Prompt" to receive a breakdown and feedback.

Step 3: Use the "Rewrite" button to answer clarifying questions and improve your prompt.

Step 4: Click the "Generate New Prompt" button to create your new and improved prompt.

This site is powered by the free tier of the Google Gemini API. Due to usage limits, you may experience delays or interruptions.
